TASTE OF SPRING LEMON BLUEBERRY CUPCAKES

Everything about spring makes me happy: the flowers, the baby birds, fresh green grass…that feeling like everything is renewing and the air is ripe with possibility. That’s what these Lemon Blueberry Cupcakes remind me of. The earthy, clean feel of spring in the air. With that tart lemon ripe on your tongue and that earthy blueberry juice drizzling down the back of your throat, I’m pretty sure you’ll feel it, too. Enjoy!
