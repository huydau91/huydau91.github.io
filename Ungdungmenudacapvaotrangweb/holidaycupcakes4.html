BRING ON SPRING BIRD’S NEST CUPCAKES

Right now there is a bird’s nest outside my bedroom window. You’d think this is cute, you know, Spring and all…but it’s loud. Really, really loud. And while I always thought birds didn’t wake up or start chirping or anything until morning, I was very very wrong. Now, right now it’s just the mama bird. She’s a squawker. LOUD. And she loves to talk. Apparently, to anyone passing by. I live on a busy street…she talks a lot. *takes a deep breath* It’s okay. Pretty soon those little chicks will hatch and then I’ll get to see some ugly little baby chicklings (they’re super ugly at first) start their new lives. It’s kind of sweet. Kind of like my own little window into nature. Not always pretty, but very entertaining. Know what IS pretty? These Bird’s Nest Cupcakes. They are stinkin’ adorable, in fact. Way better than the nest outside my window. Know what else they are? Quiet. Yeah. I kinda wish that mama bird would take the hint, but, who am I to tell nature what to do? Enjoy!
