BABY IT’S COLD OUTSIDE HOT CHOCOLATE CUPCAKES

Actually, it isn’t cold. But sometimes when I get hot I like to turn down the AC, put on a sweater, and dream of cooler days. That’s how we here in Texas make it through. Sure, sure, some of the rest of you have more luck with those cool days than we do, but this past winter we didn’t get a single snow day AND the average temperature was something like 60. How unfair is that? So you know what? I might not be able to manufacture an actual winter, but I can pretend and enjoy the tastes of winter year round. These Hot Chocolate Cupcakes are perfect for that. For one, they aren’t actually hot. For another, even during the blasting hot summer days, I can close my eyes, cup this cupcake in both hands, and dream about cooler days… Enjoy!
