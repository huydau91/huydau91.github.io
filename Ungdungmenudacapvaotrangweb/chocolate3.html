VERY BERRY CHERRY CHOCOLATE CUPCAKES

What can I say about these Very Berry Cherry Chocolate Cupcakes except that they are berry delicious. If you find yourself buried in work, or barely about to keep your head above water you should probably make these. Between the fruit and the chocolate, that sweet, sweet frosting and just the fact that you’re treating yourself well, you’ll feel better in no time. Or, maybe life is perfect, swell, just dandy. That’s cool. These are still great. I’m just saying, anytime you put berries and chocolate in the same place, toss in some cherries, and cook it all up…you’re in for a good time. Enjoy!
