BLACK TIE CHIC ELEGANT DARK CHOCOLATE CUPCAKES

Black tie galas are something that always seemed a little hoity-toity to me. Not that I wouldn’t go if invited, but I’m pretty sure I’d use the wrong fork. Not to mention the whole dressing-for-the-event and knowing-what-to-say-to-people thing. So, pretty much not my scene. That doesn’t mean don’t I think they are glamorous and exciting and thrilling. In fact, if I showed up to one and James Bond didn’t produce himself at my elbow in order to tango before ordering his famous martini, I’d be stricken. It’s okay, though. These Dark Chocolate Cupcakes are not only perfect for a formal cupcake setting, but for those weekends where I’m wearing sweatpants and dreaming of men in black tuxes. Enjoy!
