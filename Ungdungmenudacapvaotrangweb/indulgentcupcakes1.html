GETTIN’ CAUGHT IN THE RAIN PIÑA COLADA CUPCAKES

When I was about ten years old, one of my favorite songs was Do You Like Piña Coladas (Jimmy Buffet, actually called Escape) and my mother would laugh and laugh every time I’d start singing along. At that age I had no idea what the song was actually about, but now…now I get it. And you know? Sometimes I can relate. Sometimes I feel like I just need to have some sort of romantic tryst with a stranger I’ve loved for years. Sometimes I just need a little something…different. These Piña Colada Cupcakes are something just a little different, too. They’ll take you out of the norm of boring ol’ cupcakes and let you have that escape you’re craving. Sometimes you don’t actually have to get away to feel like you’re having an adventure, and these cupcakes are perfect for that. Enjoy!
